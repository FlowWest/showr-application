Using SHOWR

Management of Shasta Reservoir to protect Winter Run Chinook Salmon is driven by the Reasonable and Prudent Actions (RPAs) detailed in the 2009 Biological Opinion on the Long-Term Operations of the Central Valley Project and State Water Project. The Winter Run Chinook Salmon RPAs are primarily focused on cold water management in Shasta Reservoir, temperature thresholds in the Sacramento River downstream of Shasta, and Winter Run Chinook Salmon adult spawning, egg survival, and fry emergence.
